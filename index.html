Facilities

                                                     School embarked on its journey on April 5, 2000 with strength of 800 students.The success story of the school in such a short span of time is a testimony to the hard work of the principal, staff, parents and the students.

Sunshine Mellow Public school,Jaipur has all world class facilities such as swimming pool, tennis court, basketball court, indoor games arena, cricket and football ground, landscape park, accessible beach area, two separate canteens, two rich libraries,well-ventilated classrooms with air conditioners equipped with computers and tablets to study .

Our teaching staff consists of well-known and world-famous teachers. School focuses on the all-round development of children by instilling in them the power of thinking and providing with top class facilities.

We also have a sprawling lush green ground for outdoor activities.The school has made earnest effort to evaluate the Child's Scholastic and Co-Scholastic domains through assessments at regular intervals.French, German,Russian, Italian and Sanskrit are taught as III Language subjects in the middle classes. Apart from Scout and Guides, NCC has also been added as a part of School Curriculum.The school is affiliated to CBSE New Delhi as co-educational senior secondary school.

First batch of class XII passed out in 2002. The school offers all three streams of study Science, Commerce and Humanities.

Presently our school has a strength of 4000 students, 120 teachers and 10 sections in each class (Total 120 classes in all).
